Our Traceability System

  • All accepted materials are labeled with a unique product storage number.
  • Production records indicate the storage number, traceable from the batch number and QR code.
  • Records are signed by relevant operators and retained for a specified period.
  • Before release, all information is cross checked to the ERP system by quality assurance staff.
