Our Business

We were the first New Zealand milk powder company to be certified by the certification and accreditation administrator of the People’s Republic of China, the CNCA, to export infant formula to China.

We were also proud to be the dairy sponsor of the New Zealand Olympic Team during the period of 2016 to March 2018, supporting athletes at both the Rio Summer Olympics and the Pyeonchang Winter Olympics with dairy products.

GMP Dairy sells milk powder-based products to pharmacies, grocery, local dairy and specialty stores. Cowala has become a trusted brand recommended by friends and family within New Zealand and beyond.

Cowala is a top quality brand produced in New Zealand.

Cowala products are currently exported to Australia, China, Hong Kong, Korea, Vietnam and Malaysia.
