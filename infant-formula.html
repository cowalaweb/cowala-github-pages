COWALA Infant Formula

Cowala manufactures a range of New Zealand derived infant formula for balanced development of baby’s bones, brain and eyes.

Utilising strict pharmaceutical standards, the infant formula products are formulated to meet international standards using quality ingredients.

From Birth

Premium infant formula products designed to support bottle fed infants from 0 to 6 months old.

6-12 months

Follow-on formula products designed for the changing nutrition needs of growing babies as solids are introduced.

1-3 years

Toddler milk drink nutrition supplements formulated for young children when normal diet may not be adequate.
