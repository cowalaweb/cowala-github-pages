Why COWALA

We believe in quality above all else.We always strive for excellence in every stage of our operations. Our origins as a pharmaceutical manufacturer have resulted in very high standards of hygiene, safety and quality control. New Zealand provides the perfect conditions for the production of milk and we work with a number of local dairy companies including Fonterra, one of the world's largest, to use the best milk powder available. All our ingredients are checked, tasted and retested continuously to ensure they meet our high standard. Our products are 100% natural and contain no artificial flavours, colours or preservatives.

Our Product

Cowala and all its supplier use the best resources to produce the highest quality products, and our product formulations are strictly controlled to ensure consistent production standards are maintained.
